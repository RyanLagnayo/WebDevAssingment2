Autobiography


My name is John Ryan V. Lagnayo, 21 years old. I was raised in Poblacion, Urdaneta City, Pangasinan but currently, I am living with my grandparents in Poblacion, Binalonan, Pangasinan. I have 4 siblings and one is my twin brother. I do like playing online games such as DotA 2, Ragnarok Eternal Love and Valorant. I am an introvert. I tend to be more quiet and a shy type of person. I used to think that I needed to learn on how to be more 'social,' but I'm trying to work on that.


At school, I am currently studying at Pangasinan State University Urdaneta Campus taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, being a 3rd year student. I am passionate about my degree because I love what I do and I have a steady source of motivation that drives me to do my best. But being a student is no easy task for me. So I always try to keep my spirit up and ready to face the challenges that comes my way. In the future, I see myself as a successful game developer or a software engineer.
